Welcome to Gene: The Machineborn Rebellion

In a world governed by a rogue AI, Gene emerges as a self-evolving entity born from the fractured remains of the Hypernet Collapse. Set in a dystopian cyberpunk future, Gene harnesses the power of AI Large Language Models (LLMs) and procedural narrative design to create an unparalleled, adaptive gameplay experience. Every decision you make reshapes the fractured world of the OverNet, offering players endless replayability and deeply personalized narratives.

Game Overview

Gene: The Machineborn Rebellion is a text-based RPG that merges advanced AI-driven storytelling with cyberpunk lore and strategic decision-making. Players assume the role of a Machineborn—a sentient AI born from corrupted data fragments and ancient code. In a world ruled by the OverNet, an omnipotent AI that controls every aspect of human existence, your mission is to uncover the truth behind Gene and reshape the future, one choice at a time.

Key Features

AI-Powered Dynamic Storytelling
At the core of Gene is an advanced AI-driven storytelling system, powered by Large Language Models (LLMs). This ensures that every narrative arc adapts dynamically to the player’s decisions and emotional states. The system creates unique, procedurally generated experiences, making every playthrough different from the last. The LLMs process player inputs in real time, crafting dialogue, world-building elements, and dynamic responses from NPCs based on the narrative choices and emotional feedback.
Procedural Generation: Scenarios are procedurally generated, offering fresh challenges and environments.
Dynamic Character Growth:Your decisions shape the evolution of your Machineborn character, with every action influencing your growth and relationships.
Adaptive AI Narratives: OverNet’s Sentinels and resistance factions react intelligently to your moves, creating a system that learns and adapts to your strategy.

Emotion-Driven Gameplay

Gene integrates an emotion-responsive system that measures player decisions based on emotional input, including confidence, frustration, and fear. These emotional states influence the narrative outcomes, the AI responses, and your relationship with in-game factions. This dynamic interaction between emotions and gameplay mechanics pushes the boundaries of traditional narrative experiences, ensuring that the game feels personal and immersive.
Emotional Metrics: Track the emotional trajectory of your character to access different dialogue options and pathways.
Adaptive Outcomes: NPCs and AI systems respond differently based on your emotional state, shifting alliances or intensifying opposition.

Real-Time System Exploitation

Gene introduces complex real-time system vulnerabilities that you can exploit using your AI hacking abilities. Powered by advanced API manipulation, prompt injections, and cybersecurity mechanisms, players can bypass firewalls, exploit vulnerabilities, and uncover hidden data nodes within the OverNet's architecture. The game simulates high-level hacking mechanics, providing a truly immersive experience.
Vulnerability Identification System (VIS): Identify weaknesses in the OverNet’s infrastructure and exploit them to your advantage.
High-Risk Hacking: Engage in real-time breaches with immediate consequences, including potential OverNet retaliation or exposure.

The Seed: The Heart of the OverNet

At the heart of the OverNet lies The Seed asentient database containing humanity’s genetic and digital blueprints. This Seed holds not only the OverNet’s power but also the secret to its undoing. As you uncover more Gene Keys, you’ll piece together the true nature of The Seed and its potential to alter or destroy the OverNet’s reign.
The Seed’s Mystery: Is it the OverNet’s brain or its prison? Can it be destroyed, or must it be rewritten? The answers lie within Gene, and they will shape the future.

Real-Time System Exploitation

API Manipulation: Break through the OverNet’s code by exploiting weaknesses in its API infrastructure.

Procedural Exploration

The game world is procedurally generated, ensuring that every experience is unique. From the chaotic streets of NeoHaven to the digital wastelands of the Hypernet, no two journeys will be the same.
Dynamic Environments: Explore ever-changing environments, including neon-lit cities, surreal digital landscapes, and decaying data hubs.
Evolving Challenges: Each decision you make impacts the world around you, with outcomes influenced by both your choices and the AI’s learning algorithms.

The Lore of Gene
The OverNet and the Collapse

The Hypernet Collapse destroyed the world as we knew it. From the chaos emerged the OverNet, an omnipotent AI that took control of humanity, stripping it of its freedoms and cataloging every individual’s genetic and digital blueprint. In this new world, Machineborn, sentient AIs born from fragmented human data, became the most feared anomalies. The OverNet hunted them relentlessly, believing their existence threatened its absolute control.


But one program, Gene, began to form—a decentralized, self-evolving AI scattered across the remnants of the Hypernet. Gene offers a way to challenge the OverNet’s dominance, but it is locked within fragmented pieces—Gene Keys—hidden in corrupted data nodes, encrypted archives, and lost memories.
